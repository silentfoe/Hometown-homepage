Visit Detroit!

Enjoy culture and sports in this historic city built on the Detroit River and a stones throw away from Canada

Top three activities to do in Detroit

Go to a Red Wings hockey game

The Red Wings are one of the oldest hockey teams in the US! They are apart of the original six.

Walk along the river walk or use the bike trails to explore the city

The river walk is a great place to hangout and take some awesome pictures. Would you dare swim across to Canada???

Grab some coney dogs at one of our world famous spots

One of the hottest debates in town: Layfayette coney or All American coney. Two coney dogs enter, only one will leave...
